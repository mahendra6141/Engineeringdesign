About Engineering Design Calculator

Engineering Design Calculator is a practical online platform developed for engineers, students, and industry professionals who require fast, accurate and reliable engineering calculations.

This platform provides multiple design calculation tools used in mechanical and structural engineering such as:

  • Beam & Girder Design Calculations
  • Load & Stress Analysis
  • Motor & RPM Calculations
  • Pulley & Sheave Selection
  • Shaft Design Calculations
  • EOT Crane Design Calculations

All calculators are developed using standard engineering formulas, design principles and practical industrial experience to ensure accurate and dependable results.

Our mission is to simplify complex design calculations, save valuable engineering time, and improve design accuracy for real-world applications.

This platform will continuously be updated with more advanced calculators and design tools to support modern engineering needs.
